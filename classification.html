So am I a beginner? What comes after?

Weightlifters are classified as beginner, intermediate or advanced

Based on how long it takes to see a strength adaptation

Beginners can see increases in strength after 2-4 days.

Intermediates can see increases after a week.

Advanced trainees look at their progress on a month or year to year basis.

After strength progression tapers off, a trainer should look to change programs,

a well known intermediate program being Barbell Medicine The Bridge
