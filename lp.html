Linear Progression AKA Beginner Gains

As a person with little or no gym experience, it is possible to

increase the weight every time you go to the gym!

Note that this is only possible on compound movements

movements which incorporate multiple muscles at once, as opposed to

isolation exercises such as bicep curls, which target one muscle or area at a time

So for each exercise in the program, we will increase the weight by 5 pounds

every week until an adaptation ceases to occur.
